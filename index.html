Welcome to Sidiaboly Arabic & Islamic School!

At Sidiaboly, we are dedicated to providing an enriching educational experience rooted in the Arabic language and Islamic teachings. Our school is a nurturing environment where students of all ages can explore, learn, and grow, guided by a team of experienced and passionate educators. We understand the importance of fostering a strong foundation in Arabic and Islamic studies, as they form an integral part of cultural and religious identity.

Through a comprehensive curriculum, interactive lessons, and engaging activities, we strive to empower our students with the knowledge, skills, and values that will shape them into well-rounded individuals. Our committed faculty members create a supportive learning environment, promoting inclusivity, respect, and a love for knowledge. We believe in the potential of every student and tailor our teaching methods to meet their unique needs, ensuring that they thrive academically and spiritually. Whether you are seeking a dynamic Arabic language program or a holistic Islamic education, Sidiaboly Arabic & Islamic School is here to help you embark on a meaningful educational journey. Join us as we inspire young minds and nurture a deep appreciation for the Arabic language and Islamic traditions. Take the first step towards an extraordinary education. Explore our programs and discover the Sidiaboly difference today!

Quran Study (Intermediate Level)

In this level, students deepen their understanding of Islamic teachings and practices. They study more advanced topics in Quranic recitation, memorization of selected chapters (surahs) from the Quran, Islamic jurisprudence (fiqh), and the biography of Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him). Arabic language skills are further developed, including reading, writing, and comprehension.

Learn More
Advance Level

At this stage, students delve into more complex subjects of Islamic studies, including tafsir (Quranic exegesis), Hadith (Prophetic traditions), Islamic history, comparative religion, Islamic philosophy, and contemporary issues. They also continue Quran memorization and recitation practice. Arabic language skills are honed to facilitate the understanding of classical Islamic texts.

Arabic: اللَّهُمَّ اهْدِنَا فِيمَنْ هَدَيْتَ، وَعَافِنَا فِيمَنْ عَافَيْتَ، وَتَوَلَّنَا فِيمَنْ تَوَلَّيْتَ، وَبَارِكْ لَنَا فِيمَا أَعْطَيْتَ، وَقِنَا شَرَّ مَا قَضَيْتَ، فَإِنَّكَ تَقْضِي وَلا يُقْضَى عَلَيْكَ.
Translation: "O Allah, guide us among those whom You have guided, pardon us among those whom You have pardoned, turn to us in friendship among those on whom You have turned in friendship, and bless us in what You have bestowed, and protect us from the evil you have decreed. Verily, You decree and none can decree over You."
